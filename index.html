WIRLIXING Defined:

Wirlix provides a live exchange of ideas and opinions using videos and images with the purpose of understanding the other person’s perspective and point of view. Basically, users (you) click through images and videos depicting two friends (or you and a friend) debating on a controversial subject matter to lay it naked and bare, thus, gaining a common consensus that transcends all one-sided media bias.
